QControl Database System

Designed and Developed By: Mimi Pieper, Chandler Campbell, Noah Mogensen

QControl is a dedicated database application designed to address the quality control (QC) management needs of small-scale manufacturers. It provides digital capabilities for the efficient tracking and reporting of quality control processes, replacing traditional paper-based methods.
