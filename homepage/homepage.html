QControl

Quality Control Management System

Designed and Developed By: Mimi Pieper, Chandler Campbell, Noah Mogensen

Overview:

QControl is a specialized database application tailored for small-scale manufacturers, designed to revolutionize their quality control management. It steps in to replace outdated paper-based methods with a digital approach, significantly enhancing efficiency in tracking and reporting quality control processes.

Why It Matters:

For small and medium-sized manufacturing businesses, the options for quality management systems are often limited to complex, costly solutions or inefficient, error-prone spreadsheet-based methods. QControl fills this crucial market gap by providing a simplified, yet effective digital quality control management system. It's an ideal solution for businesses that find full-scale ERP systems excessive for their needs but seek a more efficient alternative to paper and spreadsheets.

By adopting QControl, small-scale manufacturers can significantly improve their quality control processes, leading to better product quality, reduced waste, and increased customer satisfaction. This not only optimizes their operations but also provides them with a competitive edge in the market.
